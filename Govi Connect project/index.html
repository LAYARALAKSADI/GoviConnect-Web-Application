About Govi Connect

Transforming agriculture in Dambulla through digital innovation

Our Mission

Govi Connect is a digital platform created specifically for the Dambulla Economic Center to bridge the gap between farmers and buyers. We aim to eliminate intermediaries and ensure fair prices for agricultural products.

Our platform empowers farmers with direct market access, real-time pricing information, and secure payment systems while providing buyers with quality produce directly from source.

By leveraging technology, we're creating a transparent, efficient, and sustainable agricultural ecosystem that benefits all stakeholders in the supply chain.
